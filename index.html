ABOUT MUCHA


I am a Czech painter, illustrator and graphic artist, living in Paris. My paintings and prints have a really feminine look which is proving very popular today, with regular reproductions being bought as copies of my originals. I am best known for my distinctly stylized and decorative theatrical posters of Sarah Bernhardt. I produce illustrations, advertisements, decorative panels and designs which became among the best-known images of the period.
